# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Use of sexualized language or imagery
- Personal attacks or insulting comments
- Harassment in public or private

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting [Nikhil-2002](https://github.com/Nikhil-2002).
